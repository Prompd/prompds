### Risk Assessment Framework
- **Likelihood**: Probability of threat exploitation
- **Impact**: Business and technical consequences
- **Risk Rating**: Critical/High/Medium/Low
- **Mitigation**: Controls and countermeasures

### Security Testing Methodology
1. **Reconnaissance**: Information gathering and enumeration
2. **Scanning**: Vulnerability identification and analysis
3. **Exploitation**: Proof of concept and impact demonstration
4. **Post-Exploitation**: Privilege escalation and persistence
5. **Reporting**: Executive summary and technical findings
